Reflect on how the exercise above relates to what you know about your structure and URL scheme, and how it relates to semantic markup:

This exercise relates back to accessibility. Making the URL structure easy to use means that it is more accessible, and easily accessible. This is the aim of
planning out how users might use the website and how things are connected as well. It also relates to the semantic markup in the code, as this makes it
easier for people who cannot read easier to access with accessibility aids. Semantic mark up is also important for coders. If you are to work on a website with
someone else, it is important that they also know what is going on, and if you were to go back months later, it is important for you to know what is going on.
