Reflect on your work for Website 1: Where have you struggled?

I am and have struggled with some of the more pragmatic decisions. Above I have a basic information structure and user flow, but I am not sure if these are how I want them to be, or if they are w=effective the way they are now. This is something I want to discuss with a tutor or with Hanli in class, so i can make some changes before I hand in my first website. i am also not sure how much of this I can implement with the skills I have now, I will have to look into it further.

What are you proud of?

I am proud of how quickly I was able to figure out how to do many of the things I wanted to implement in my website to this point. Yes, these are simple things, but I was really afraid that I was not going to know what goes on most of the time, or that I would struggle a lot more than I initially thought I would. And do not get me wrong, none of this is easy by any means, but i enjoy it, and I am impressed by how much of it I understand.

What do you want to change going forward?

The way the website looks right now isn't all that great, and I would like to be able to change that soon, and make it look a bit more like I want it to. There is still a lot I want to implement and I am not able to do some of it yet, but I am excited to learn how to do so.
